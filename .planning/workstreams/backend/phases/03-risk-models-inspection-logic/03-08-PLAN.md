<objective>
Wire provenance confidence into production risk recomputation and fix model correctness issues.

Purpose: The BLOCKER gap where the weak-evidence floor (D-09) is disconnected in production — risk_service hardcodes provenance_confidence: None — means low-confidence structures never get the "inspection required" floor. This plan loads the structure's ProvenanceModel and passes its confidence_level to risk_engine.compute_risk, closing the gap. Additionally fixes model barrel exports, CheckConstraints, and timezone-aware datetime defaults that cause warnings and autogenerate drift.

Output: Production risk recomputation triggers the weak-evidence floor when provenance is LOW; all models correctly exported and constrained.
</objective>
